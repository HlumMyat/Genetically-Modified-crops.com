Higher Crop Yield

GM crops often produce more food per plant, helping farmers feed more people.

Pest and Disease Resistance

Some GM crops make their own natural protection, so farmers can use fewer pesticides.

Drought and Climate Tolerance

They can survive harsh weather conditions like drought, heat, or poor soil.

Better Nutrition

Scientists can add vitamins or minerals to crops, such as Golden Rice with vitamin A to fight blindness.

Economic Advantages

Farmers can spend less on chemicals and grow more efficiently, improving their income.
